About Us

Home / About

Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to excellence in precision machining.

Founded in 2000, Nidhi Mechanical Works has grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of precision-engineered shafts and travelers. Our journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence and innovation in the field of precision machining.

With decades of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking high-quality machined components. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced machinery and staffed by skilled technicians who are passionate about their craft.

Today, we continue to build on our legacy of quality and precision, serving clients across various industries with our expertise in iron turning, rough cutting, center removal, final finishing, keyway cutting, grinding, and hole drilling.
