Our Services

Home / Services

Comprehensive precision machining services tailored to your specific requirements.

From concept to completion, we provide end-to-end manufacturing services tailored to your specific requirements.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality shafts, travelers, and custom machined components for various industrial applications.

Shaft Turning

Our shaft turning service utilizes advanced lathes to produce precision shafts for various industrial applications. We can manufacture shafts in a wide range of dimensions and materials, ensuring tight tolerances and excellent surface finish.

  • Precision lathes turning
  • Diameters from 10mm to 250mm
  • Lengths up to 1500mm
  • Thread cutting
Learn More

Center & Facecut

Our center and facecut services provide precise preparation of shaft ends for various applications. We ensure accurate centering and face cutting to meet your specific requirements, whether for further machining operations or final product specifications.

  • Preparation for further machining
  • Various center types
  • High precision and repeatability
  • Accurate face cutting
Learn More

Keyway Machining

Our keyway machining service provides precise cutting of keyways in shafts. We can produce standard and custom keyways to meet your specific requirements, ensuring accurate dimensions and positioning.

  • Standard and custom keyways
  • Accurate positioning
  • Multiple keyways on single shaft
  • Various keyway types
Learn More

Grinding & Polishing

Our grinding and polishing services provide superior surface finish and dimensional accuracy for your components. We utilize advanced grinding equipment to achieve the highest level of precision and surface quality for critical applications.

  • Mirror-like surface finish
  • Various material capabilities

Hole Drilling

Our precision hole drilling service provides accurate and consistent holes for various applications. We can drill holes of different diameters and depths in various materials, ensuring precise positioning and dimensional accuracy.

  • Precision hole positioning
  • Various hole diameters
