Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What is your typical lead time for orders?

Our typical lead time varies depending on the complexity and quantity of the order. For standard products, we usually deliver within 2-3 weeks. We'll provide you with a specific timeline when we quote your project.

Do you offer custom shafting for rope machines?

Yes, we specialize in custom lathe work for rope machine shafting. We manufacture shafts to exact specifications, ensuring durability, precise tolerance, and long-lasting performance.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We don't have a strict minimum order quantity. We can handle everything from single prototypes to large production runs. However, pricing may vary based on quantity due to setup costs.

Can I bring my own design or sample for jobwork?

Absolutely! We accept both design drawings and physical samples. Our team will replicate or customize the shaft as per your requirements with high accuracy using precision turning methods.

Can you create shafts with keyways, threads, or specific grooves?

Absolutely! We offer complete machining services including keyway cutting, threading (internal and external), grooving, taper turning, and knurling as per client requirements.
